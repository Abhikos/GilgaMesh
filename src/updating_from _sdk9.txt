updating from sdk9

build with cmake
..../_build$ cmake -DCMAKE_BUILD_TYPE=Debug -DIS_TEST_BUILD=false ../
..../_build$ make

$> JlinkExe
connect, swd, 4000

restore factory settings in order to be able to change the boot address:
J-Link> w4 4001e504 2
J-Link> w4 4001e50c 1

flash the softdevice:
J-Link> loadfile s130_nrf51_2.0.1_softdevice.hex (under deploy/softdevice)

flash the sdk11 version of meshymesh (this is the only step required when doing incremental builds):
J-Link> loadfile MeshyMesh.hex (under _build/Debug)

verify with getting the command console:
(while JlinkExe is connected in an other window)
$> JlinkRTTClient (should show Connected to process: JLinkExe)

(switch back to the JLinkExe window and reset and go)
J-Link> r
J-Link> g

You sould now see the MeshyMesh boot messages on the JLinkRTTClient window
